Whose Tap?

A community-narrative map of California's drinking water crisis
Stories
All systems
Prototype. Data: California State Water Resources Control Board SAFER Drinking Water Tracker; CalEnviroScreen 4.0; public reporting (LA Times, Visalia Times-Delta, ProPublica, Community Water Center, AGUA Coalition).

Stories on this prototype are paraphrased from public reporting — in production they would be authored by the community, with the community retaining ownership of voice, photograph, and audio.

Open questions for the partner:
  • Who hosts this and pays the bill in year 3?
  • Who controls edits, takedowns, and the right to be forgotten?
  • Offline / low-bandwidth access for community members?
  • Translation by whom — Spanish, Mixteco, Hmong, Punjabi?
  • What about communities that don't want to be on the map?
